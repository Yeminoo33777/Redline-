Controls & Tips

Keyboard & Mouse

  • W/A/S/D – Move
  • Space – Jump
  • Shift (hold) – Sprint
  • R – Reload
  • Left Click – Shoot (hold for auto)
  • Right Click – Toggle scope
  • ESC – Unlock mouse

Game Rules

  • 💥 You have 100 HP. Bots deal 1 damage per hit (multiple bots = faster death).
  • 🔫 Your AK‑47 holds 30 bullets. Reload takes 1.2 seconds.
  • 🎯 Headshots kill bots instantly. Body shots require 3 hits.
  • 🤖 New bots spawn as you kill them – infinite waves!
  • 🗺️ Use the minimap to track enemy positions.

Browser Compatibility

Works best on Chrome, Firefox, or Edge with pointer lock enabled. If the game doesn't capture your mouse, click anywhere on the page after it loads.
