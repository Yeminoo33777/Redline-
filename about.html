About Redline Single Player

This is a fan‑made, single‑player version of the popular browser game Krunker.io. It's built entirely with Three.js and vanilla JavaScript, demonstrating first‑person shooter mechanics in a 3D web environment.

The project started as a learning experiment and grew into a playable demo featuring AI bots, a custom AK‑47 model, a live minimap, and visual effects like hit flashes and scoping.

Behind the Scenes

  • Engine: Three.js (r160) for 3D rendering.
  • Bots: Simple state machine with obstacle avoidance (sliding along walls).
  • Weapon: Hand‑modeled AK‑47 with a scope, built from primitive shapes.
  • UI: Dynamic HUD with health bar, ammo counter, and minimap (canvas 2D).
  • Sound: Placeholder audio files for shooting, reloading, hits, and footsteps.

Why Single Player?

Krunker.io is an intense multiplayer game. This version lets you practice aim, learn movement, and enjoy the mechanics without the pressure of real opponents. It's also fully open‑source – you can inspect every line of code.

“I built this to understand game development with Three.js. Feel free to fork, modify, or contribute!” – Developer
